
Controls:
  Hold the left mouse button down on the window and use the mouse to look around.
  
  WASD are used for movement.

  E switches between first and third person view.
  
  Q disconnects the view from the character and allows free movement within the scene.  Use WASD to move the camera and IJKL to move the character.

References:
  Alot of procedural content generation found at http://pcg.wikidot.com/

  Basic maze/cavern creation found at http://properundead.com/2009/03/cave-generator.html

  Fractal perlin noise generator from http://freespace.virgin.net/hugo.elias/models/m_perlin.htm

  Models taken from http://e2-productions.com/repository/
